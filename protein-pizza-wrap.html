High-Protein Pizza Wrap Recipe

Description

Indulge in a culinary delight that's not just delicious but also nutritionally balanced with our High-Protein Pizza Wrap recipe. Crafted for those who don't want to compromise on taste while adhering to a health-conscious diet, this wrap combines the succulent flavors of cooked chicken breast, melted low-fat mozzarella, and a smattering of fresh veggies, all enveloped in a wholesome whole wheat tortilla. With approximately 453 calories, this wrap packs a whopping 48.7 grams of protein, ensuring you meet your nutritional goals without feeling deprived.



The preparation is as straightforward as it is quick. A smattering of tomato sauce serves as the base, topped with a generous layer of shredded chicken breast for that protein punch. Fresh spinach and red bell pepper slices add a burst of color and nutrients, while a sprinkle of low-fat mozzarella cheese brings that much-loved pizza flair. Seasoned with a blend of garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper, the wrap is then folded and cooked to golden-brown perfection. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a post-workout meal, this High-Protein Pizza Wrap is a guilt-free treat that satisfies both your taste buds and nutritional needs.



Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Tortilla (1 medium-sized, around 8 inches) - 120 calories, 4g protein, 2g fat, 22g carbs
  • Chicken Breast, cooked and shredded (4 oz) - 180 calories, 35g protein, 4g fat, 0g carbs
  • Low-Fat Mozzarella Cheese (1/4 cup) - 80 calories, 8g protein, 5g fat, 1g carbs
  • Tomato Sauce (1/4 cup) - 20 calories, 1g protein, 0g fat, 5g carbs
  • Spinach (1/2 cup, fresh) - 3 calories, 0.4g protein, 0g fat, 0.4g carbs
  • Red Bell Pepper (1/4 cup, sliced) - 10 calories, 0.3g protein, 0g fat, 2g carbs
  • Olive Oil (1 tsp) - 40 calories, 0g protein, 4.5g fat, 0g carbs
  • Spices: Garlic powder, Oregano, Salt, and Pepper to taste

Steps

  • Preparation: Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add 1 tsp of olive oil.
  • Cook Veggies: Toss in the sliced red bell peppers and fresh spinach. Cook until the veggies are soft. Remove them from the pan and set aside.
  • Assemble Wrap: Place the whole wheat tortilla flat on a clean surface. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the tortilla.
  • Add Toppings: Add the shredded chicken, cooked veggies, and low-fat mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  • Fold and Cook: Carefully fold the tortilla to make a wrap. Place the wrap back into the pan and cook until each side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  • Serve: Once cooked, remove the wrap from the pan, slice it in half, and enjoy your high-protein pizza wrap!
