High-Protein Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Description

Kickstart your day or refuel post-workout with this High-Protein Smoothie, a creamy blend that's as delicious as it is nutritious. Packed with 20 grams of protein from protein powder and an additional 6 grams from other ingredients, this smoothie ensures you meet your protein needs in the tastiest way possible. Almond milk and fresh spinach provide a low-calorie base, while a banana adds natural sweetness and fiber. A dollop of peanut butter brings a creamy texture and a touch of healthy fats. Totalling around 332 calories, 27 grams of protein, 12 grams of fat, and 34 grams of carbs, this smoothie is a balanced and delicious option for any time of the day.



Ingredients

  • Protein Powder (1 scoop) - 100 calories, 20g protein, 1g fat, 2g carbs
  • Almond Milk (1 cup) - 30 calories, 1g protein, 2.5g fat, 1g carbs
  • Banana (1 medium) - 105 calories, 1g protein, 0g fat, 27g carbs
  • Peanut Butter (1 tbsp) - 90 calories, 4g protein, 8g fat, 3g carbs
  • Spinach (1 cup, fresh) - 7 calories, 1g protein, 0g fat, 1g carbs
  • Ice Cubes (1 cup)

Steps

  • Prepare Ingredients: Measure all the ingredients and keep them ready.
  • Blend Greens: In a blender, first blend the spinach and almond milk until smooth.
  • Add Fruits and Protein: Add the banana and protein powder to the blender.
  • Add Fats: Add the peanut butter for a creamy texture and additional protein.
  • Blend: Blend all the ingredients until smooth.
  • Ice It: Add ice cubes and blend again briefly.
  • Serve: Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy!
