High-Protein Quinoa Bowl Recipe

Description

Revel in the flavors and nutrients of the Protein-Packed Quinoa Bowl, a dish designed for both taste and health. This bowl combines the nutty essence of quinoa with the lean protein of grilled chicken, all complemented by the creamy texture of avocado and the heartiness of black beans. Fresh cherry tomatoes and spinach add a burst of color and nutrients, while a drizzle of lemon juice brings a zesty finish. With a balanced profile of approximately 602 calories, 51.5 grams of protein, 16 grams of fat, and 61 grams of carbs, this quinoa bowl is a powerhouse meal that keeps you fueled without weighing you down.



Ingredients

  • Quinoa (1/2 cup, uncooked) - 160 calories, 6g protein, 2.5g fat, 30g carbs
  • Grilled Chicken Breast (4 oz) - 180 calories, 35g protein, 4g fat, 0g carbs
  • Avocado (1/2 medium) - 120 calories, 1.5g protein, 11g fat, 6g carbs
  • Black Beans (1/2 cup, cooked) - 120 calories, 7g protein, 0.5g fat, 20g carbs
  • Cherry Tomatoes (1/2 cup) - 15 calories, 1g protein, 0g fat, 3g carbs
  • Spinach (1 cup, fresh) - 7 calories, 1g protein, 0g fat, 1g carbs
  • Lemon Juice and Spices to taste

Steps

  • Cook Quinoa: Follow package instructions to cook quinoa.
  • Grill Chicken: Season the chicken breast and grill until fully cooked.
  • Prepare Veggies: Slice avocado and cherry tomatoes, and rinse spinach.
  • Assemble Bowl: In a bowl, combine cooked quinoa, grilled chicken, avocado slices, black beans, cherry tomatoes, and spinach.
  • Season: Drizzle with lemon juice and add spices to taste.
  • Serve: Enjoy your protein-packed quinoa bowl!
